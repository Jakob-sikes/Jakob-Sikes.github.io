3D Models

This collection of 3D weapon variations was designed for product visualization, focusing on creating a futuristic, off-world aesthetic that diverges from traditional firearm designs. Modeled and textured in Blender, I utilized mirror modifiers to refine symmetrical structures and incorporated Bloom effects to enhance the glowing elements, adding depth and variation. The designs emphasize sleek yet unconventional forms, pushing beyond the orthodox silhouettes of modern weaponry.

This 3D jet model, inspired by the X-Wing from Star Wars, was created as a product visualization piece to explore intricate mechanical design and animation. Modeled and textured in Blender, the project emphasizes both aesthetics and functionality, featuring animated landing gear retraction and wing separation to highlight its complexity. A carefully crafted lighting setup and a turntable animation enhance the presentation, creating a striking and immersive showcase that amplifies the futuristic appeal.
