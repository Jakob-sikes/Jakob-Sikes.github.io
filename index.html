Projects

3D Models

This collection of 3D weapon variations was designed for product visualization, focusing on creating a futuristic, off-world aesthetic that diverges from traditional firearm designs. Modeled and textured in Blender, I utilized mirror modifiers to refine symmetrical structures and incorporated Bloom effects to enhance the glowing elements, adding depth and variation. The designs emphasize sleek yet unconventional forms, pushing beyond the orthodox silhouettes of modern weaponry.

This 3D jet model, inspired by the X-Wing from Star Wars, was created as a product visualization piece to explore intricate mechanical design and animation. Modeled and textured in Blender, the project emphasizes both aesthetics and functionality, featuring animated landing gear retraction and wing separation to highlight its complexity. A carefully crafted lighting setup and a turntable animation enhance the presentation, creating a striking and immersive showcase that amplifies the futuristic appeal.

This 3D model set features two distinct tank variations, differing in weaponry and material design while maintaining the recognizable silhouette of a generic tank. Created in Blender for product visualization, the models use relatively low-poly construction to optimize performance while retaining visual appeal—making them versatile for both static renders and real-time applications. Bloom effects and dynamic lighting enhance the presentation, emphasizing the differences in armament and material. Originally designed as a standalone piece, one of the models was later integrated into my interactive comic project, demonstrating its adaptability in different media.

Animations

This animation depicts a malfunctioning machine tearing open a portal, pulling nearby boxes into its swirling void. Shot from a static camera perspective, the scene focuses on the chaos unfolding, with added camera shake to intensify the severity of the malfunction. Modeled and textured in Blender, I utilized node shaders to create a dynamic portal illusion, enhanced by keyframed rotations for a mesmerizing spinning effect. To push the animation further, I refined it in DaVinci Resolve, applying additional effects to heighten the atmosphere and immersion. This project was a personal exploration of visual storytelling and dynamic motion, blending 3D animation with post-processing for a more polished and dramatic presentation.
