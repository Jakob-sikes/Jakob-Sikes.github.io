Bar

This bar scene was created with a warm, rustic aesthetic in mind. I UV unwrapped the wooden countertop and applied a custom texture, carefully mapping it to specific faces. The scene includes bottles, wine glasses, shot glasses, and a “Bar Rules” sign to add detailed, lived-in elements.
